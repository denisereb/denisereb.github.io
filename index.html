About my Family

I am the middle child, I have an older sister and a younger brother. Both of my parents are from Honduras, my mom is from the capital, Tegucigalpa and my dad is from a small village close to the coast called Tela. My dad came to the U.S. when he was 22 years old and my mom came when she was 19 years old. They had my older sister in Palm Beach, Florida, but even before she turned one they had moved to Indiana. Four years after they had me. When I was ten, my parents had my little brother.

About me

I was born on October 26, 2000. I learned English as my second language when I went to kindergarten. I enjoyed playing sports and doing anything that dealt with art, including posters or just as simple doodling. I absolutely loved playing with legos, I would just build houses , after building them I would disassemble them and make a new one. Growing up I was a very shy kid, I would not talk to anyone unless they talked to me first. Now as a high school junior, I have come a long way. I am not any close to shy as I was before, at times I am probably the opposite now. I am very involved with my school. I am captain of the girlsâ€™ soccer varsity team. I have been playing soccer since fifth grade making it my seventh year of playing soccer. I am also assistant coach for middle school soccer. During the school year I would always stay after to help my coach in the concession stand. As a student athlete I am always busy, attending practice everyday and making sure I was caught up with my school work. Although with everything in my schedule I have been able to keep my GPA to a 3.7. I am not only an athlete but I am also an Artist. My room is basically a museum of all my artwork. I love painting with acrylic and watercolor, but they both have there own way of using them. I also love drawing, mostly doodling, I am an amazing doodler.

How I became Interested in Computers

I have learned how to use cameras, how to edit pictures in photoshop and how to edit videos on Adobe premiere. I have also learned how to use Adobe Illustrator to create logos and garageband to create music files. I learned all of that thanks to a program called Campecine Youth Academy by Latino Youth Collective. I took that program during two of my summers and I am glad I did. Last year my interest in computers raised after that. In ninth grade I took computer science and I loved how we solved problems using algorithms.
